The Most Qualified Movie Critic Around (not really)

Ashley's Corner is the correct place for a different perspective on movies. I like to believe that I have a different outlook on life; that I look at the world in a way I like to call "a healthy middle". I try my best to see from everyone's point of view and try to understand the choices they make even if I do not agree with them. I will be honest and admit that I am judgemental and I turn everything into a joke but other than that I am a qualified movie critic.

Join me as I judge movies that are definitely more interesting than my own life.
